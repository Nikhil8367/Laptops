About Laptops

Laptops are portable computers that combine the functionality of a desktop computer with the convenience of mobility. They typically include a display screen, keyboard, and touchpad or pointing device. Laptops come in various sizes and specifications ranging from lightweight ultrabooks to powerful gaming laptops. They often feature built-in speakers, webcams, and connectivity options such as USB ports and Wi-Fi. Laptops are versatile devices for work, entertainment, and communication on the go.
